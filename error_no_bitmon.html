This tutorial will help you with the error message of there is no bitmon in your wallet.

Fixing the mistake

First let's go to the users page and log in.

Next scroll down to the bottom of the page where you would see the update button.

Click on the update button if it gives a success promt should work now in game.

Troubleshoot

If you did the steps above and you can't still log in lets do some troubleshoot.

Are you loging in with the correct email and password?

Try double checking.

Are your bitmons unlisted?

Please unlist at least one bitmon if you want to play the game.
